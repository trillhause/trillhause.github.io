Bio


Hello! I'm Millin, 2B Mechatronics Engineering student at University of Waterloo. I am a design oriented engineer and programmer who is interested in working on revolutionary products. I believe in delivering amazing user experiences using visually ecstatic design.

As a mechatronics major, my speciality lies in integrating hardware and software to create a seamless experience in electromechanical system. My program mainly focuses on mechanical design, electrical circuits and computer control systems.

History


Co-op at University of Waterloo provides an amazing opportunity for students to try out different types of jobs before graduating. So far I have done two co-ops and they differ vastly. One was designing interfaces for a software company and other was manufacturing components for a Formula SAE race car.

Winter 2016: I worked at WeMesh over the winter 2016 term. I was involved in developing the a VR client for WeMesh. This was my first job as a software developer and I learned a lot from the experience.

Summer 2015: I worked as an UX/UI designer at Kik where I worked on some really great projects which increased daily user registration for kik messenger. I worked with designers, developers and product managers on projects/products to improve overall user experience of Kik messenger. I did this to follow my passion in designing and to get an overview of what life is like a designer.

Winter 2014: Prior to doing a co-op at Kik, I did a co-op term at University of Waterloo Formula Motorsports as a Mechanical engineering co-op. I designed CAD models for complex vehicle components and manufactured them as required. The motivation behind this job was my curiosity at working in a workshop.
